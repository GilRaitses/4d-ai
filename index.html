Glacial Geophysics AI

Geospatial AI and Physics-Informed Machine Learning for Environmental Modeling

About This Project

Glacial Geophysics AI is an open research initiative focused on reconstructing past glacial landscapes using remote sensing, geospatial AI, and physics-informed machine learning. The project integrates LiDAR, satellite imagery, bathymetric surveys, and deep learning to model ice sheet movements, landscape evolution, and conservation impacts over time. We are currently seeking funding, partnerships, and contributors from nonprofit, commercial, and government sectors to expand this research into an interactive AI-powered tool for climate science and conservation.
